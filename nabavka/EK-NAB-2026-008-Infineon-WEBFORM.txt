RFQ: XMC4400 + AURIX TC397 for modular EV charging

We are developing modular EV charging infrastructure for electric bus fleets, built around Infineon components.

Our architecture:
- EK3 module (3.3kW): XMC4400 for LLC control (HRPWM)
- Charger level: TC397 for ROJ coordination (multi-module, multi-charger)
- Software: JEZGRO - custom microkernel RTOS, portable across Cortex-M4 and TriCore

Current status:
- JEZGRO running on STM32G474 (Renode) and TC397XP (custom emulator)
- XMC4400 port planned (same Cortex-M4 core)
- Hardware needed to validate against real silicon
