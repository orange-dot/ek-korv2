# Incubators & Accelerators Strategy

## Overview

This document maps relevant incubators and accelerators for Elektrokombinacija's EK3 modular charging infrastructure. Programs are categorized by tier based on investment size, network value, and strategic fit.

**Key Positioning:** Hardware cleantech startup with patent-pending modular EV charging technology (3.3kW modules scaling to 3MW).

---

## Tier 1: Global Premier Programs

### Y Combinator (YC)
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Location** | San Francisco, USA |
| **Investment** | $500,000 |
| **Equity** | 7% + incremental |
| **Duration** | 3 months (April-June for Spring batch) |
| **Deadline** | February 9, 2026 (Spring 2026 batch) |
| **Decision** | By March 13, 2026 |

**Requirements:**
- 1-minute founder video
- Written application (problem, solution, traction)
- Founders must have ≥10% equity
- Working demo strongly preferred
- Non-US companies must incorporate parent in US, Canada, Singapore, or Cayman Islands

**Fit for EK3:**
- ✅ YC actively funds hardware startups (105+ companies)
- ✅ "Requests for Startups" includes physical infrastructure for AI/clean energy
- ✅ Strong network for US market entry
- ⚠️ Requires US incorporation

**Links:** [Apply to YC](https://www.ycombinator.com/apply) | [Hardware Startups](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/industry/hardware)
