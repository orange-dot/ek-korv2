## EU Funding Ecosystem Note

European accelerators often connect to broader EU funding:
- **Horizon Europe** - Research grants
- **EIC Accelerator** - €0.5-2.5M grants + equity
- **InvestEU** - Guarantee programs
- **National programs** - Country-specific funding

Joining EU accelerators = easier access to these programs.
