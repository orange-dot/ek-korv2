> **Last Updated**: 2026-01-20
> **Status**: Complete

## Executive Summary

Rockstart Energy has **64+ portfolio companies** focused on the energy transition. Their EV charging investments are primarily **software/SaaS focused** (eDRV, Bia, Soolutions), leaving a gap for **hardware innovation** like EK3. This positions us uniquely as a hardware-first company with strong digital components.

---

## EV Charging & Transport Portfolio Companies

### Direct Competitors/Comparables

| Company | Focus | Stage | Year | Comparison to EK3 |
|---------|-------|-------|------|-------------------|
| **eDRV** | SaaS for EV charging networks (OCPP APIs) | Seed | 2020 | Software only - EK3 is hardware+software |
| **Bia Power Grid** | Hardware-agnostic EV charging analytics | Seed | 2020 | Analytics layer - EK3 is infrastructure |
| **Lanterne** | Automated task scheduling for EVs | Pre-Seed | 2021 | Fleet management - EK3 is charging hardware |
| **Soolutions** | EV charging marketplace/e-commerce | Seed | 2023 | Retail - EK3 is depot/fleet focused |

### Key Insight

**No hardware-focused EV charging company in portfolio.** All current EV investments are:
- Software/SaaS platforms
- Analytics and data services
- Marketplace/e-commerce

**EK3 fills the hardware gap** while providing strong digital components (ROJ, V2G, smart charging).

## Investment Themes from Portfolio

### Rockstart's 4 D's Framework

| D | Portfolio Evidence | EK3 Alignment |
|---|-------------------|---------------|
| **Decentralization** | Distributed energy, local management | ROJ swarm intelligence |
| **Decarbonization** | Renewables, EVs, efficiency | Bus fleet electrification |
| **Digitalization** | SaaS, APIs, smart systems | V2G, smart charging |
| **Transport** | EV charging software | Core focus - depot charging |

### Observed Gaps in Portfolio

1. **No hardware-heavy EV charging** - All software focused
2. **No bus/fleet depot focus** - Consumer/general charging
3. **No modular architecture** - Traditional approaches
4. **No V2G hardware** - Only software layer

**EK3 fills all four gaps.**
