## Program Structure

### Two Tracks

#### 1. Ideation Track
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Stage** | Early development, market positioning |
| **Grant** | €20,000 at entry |
| **Focus** | MVP development, initial validation |

#### 2. Scale-up Track
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Stage** | Proven market traction |
| **Grant** | Up to €50,000 at entry |
| **Focus** | Growth acceleration, international expansion |

### Co-Investment Grants (Both Tracks)
- **Matching**: 1:1 with private investor
- **Maximum**: €300,000 (minus at-entry grant)
- **Timeline**: During program + 9 months after
- **Eligible investors**: VCs, angel investors

**Example**: If you raise €200K from a VC, Katapult matches with €200K grant = €400K total.

## Eligibility Requirements

### Company Requirements
| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Age** | ≤6 years old at application |
| **Ownership** | ≥80% privately owned |
| **Size** | Micro or small enterprise |
| **Founder Control** | >50% owned by founding team |
| **Location** | Serbia (or willing to relocate) |

### For International Applicants
- Must register business in Serbia
- Establish significant operating base
- Key team members present in Serbia
- **Good option for EK3** - Already based in Serbia

### Smart Specialization Priority Areas
At least 50% of funding reserved for:
1. **Information and Communication Technologies** (AI emphasis)
2. **Machines and production processes of the future** ← EK3 fits here

Women co-founders specifically encouraged.

## Application Process

### Timeline (2025 Example)
| Stage | Date |
|-------|------|
| **Application Open** | ~August |
| **Deadline** | October 13, 2025, 12:00 |
| **Pre-selection** | October-November |
| **Live Pitches** | November-December |
| **Results** | December |
| **Program Start** | Q1 following year |

**Note**: One public call per year. Check [inovacionifond.rs](https://www.inovacionifond.rs/en/programs/katapult/public-call) for 2026 dates.

### Selection Process

#### Stage 1: Eligibility Check
- Basic requirements verification
- Document completeness

#### Stage 2: Pre-selection
- Selection Committee review (3 external experts)
- Written assessment by subject matter expert
- Scoring based on:
  - Innovation level
  - Market potential
  - Team capability
  - Technical feasibility

#### Stage 3: Live Pitch
- Pre-selected applicants pitch to Selection Committee
- Track assignment (Ideation vs Scale-up)
- Final ranking and selection
