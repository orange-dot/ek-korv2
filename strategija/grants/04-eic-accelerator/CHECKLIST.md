## Eligibility Verification

- [ ] Company registered in EU member state (Netherlands ✓)
- [ ] SME status (under 250 employees, <€50M turnover)
- [ ] Not received EIC Accelerator before
- [ ] Technology at TRL 5-6 minimum (or path to)
- [ ] For-profit company

## Common EIC Questions

1. Why can't a well-funded competitor do this?
2. What happens if adoption is slow?
3. How do you make money from open source?
4. What's your unfair advantage?
5. Why does your team win?
6. What are the three biggest risks?
7. How will you use the €2.5M specifically?
8. What's your Series A plan?
