# NWO Netherlands - Application Checklist

**Program:** NWO (via academic partner)
**Type:** Industry-Academic Partnership
**Role:** Industry Partner
**Status:** REQUIRES ACADEMIC PARTNER

---

## Our Role

Elektrokombinacija is the **industry partner**, not the applicant:
- Academic partner applies to NWO
- We provide use case, hardware, co-supervision
- We benefit from research output

## Phase 1: Partner Identification

- [ ] Research TU Delft groups (Embedded Systems, Power Grids)
- [ ] Research TU Eindhoven groups (Power Electronics)
- [ ] Research University of Twente (Embedded Systems)
- [ ] Identify potential supervisors with matching interests
- [ ] Leverage PhD advisor network for introductions

## Phase 2: Relationship Building

- [ ] Send introduction email to potential partners
- [ ] Schedule initial meetings
- [ ] Present EK-KOR2 technology and research needs
- [ ] Discuss potential PhD topics
- [ ] Assess mutual interest and fit

## Phase 3: Collaboration Agreement

- [ ] Agree on research scope
- [ ] Define Elektrokombinacija's contribution (in-kind)
- [ ] Clarify IP arrangements
- [ ] Sign collaboration letter / MoU

## Phase 4: Support Application

- [ ] Provide technical input for proposal
- [ ] Write letter of support
- [ ] Confirm in-kind contribution
- [ ] Review proposal draft

## Phase 5: If Funded

- [ ] Participate in PhD supervision
- [ ] Provide hardware and test environment
- [ ] Host internship periods
- [ ] Integrate results into products
