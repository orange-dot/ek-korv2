## Key Assumptions

### Pricing:
- EK3 module: EUR 1,500 (Year 2) → EUR 1,350 (Year 3) - volume
- BOM cost: EUR 800 → EUR 650 (volume discounts)
- Gross margin target: 50%+ at scale

### Market:
- EU bus fleet electrification accelerating
- 3-5 pilot customers in Year 2
- 10-15 commercial customers in Year 3

### Team:
- Serbia-based engineering (cost advantage)
- Netherlands HQ (commercial, IP)
- Competitive EU salaries for key hires

### Risks & Mitigations:

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Slow customer adoption | Start with pilot-friendly transit operators |
| Component supply issues | Multiple qualified suppliers |
| Competition response | OSS moat, speed to market |
| Technical delays | Conservative timeline, buffer built in |
