# ELEKTROKOMBINACIJA
## Open-Source Coordination for European EV Infrastructure

---

## THE OPPORTUNITY

**EU Policy:** Zero-emission city buses by 2035 (Clean Vehicles Directive)
**Current Problem:** 70-85% charger uptime, proprietary vendor lock-in
**European Gap:** No open standard for distributed energy coordination

---

## THE SOLUTION

### Two Innovations:

**1. EK-KOR2: Open-Source Coordination RTOS**
- Potential field scheduling (no central coordinator)
- Self-organizing distributed consensus
- MIT License - European-developed
- Dual C/Rust implementation

**2. EK3: Modular Power Hardware**
- 3.3 kW module, 200x320x44mm
- 900V SiC MOSFETs, LLC topology
- >96% efficiency, hot-swappable
- Scales from 3kW to 3MW

**Combined:** Self-healing infrastructure - failed module detected, load redistributed, robot-swapped in 40 seconds.
